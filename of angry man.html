What is WBBC?

Westlake BioBank for Chinese(WBBC) project will analysis and study of the genome data related to human disease health. The project will provide accurate and reliable prediction methods and decision-making basis for disease screening, diagnosis, prevention, control and treatment. And it will provide a more accurate treatment for the individual patient. Through the establishment of DNA sample library and database, to provide services for all areas of society. Nowadays Birth-defect, chronic disease and therioma have become the most serious kinds of disease affecting the health of the people with the development of medical technology. These diseases are highly inherited. So it is urgent to carry out a large-scale epidemiological investigation and genetic research to find out the etiology and pathogenesis of the disease.
