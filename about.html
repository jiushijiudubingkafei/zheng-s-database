Project design

This project is aimed at establishing a resource data base for the collection of epidemiological data and clinical case information resources, and preserving blood, urine, feces of the biological samples.



Research cohort for adolescents:

By setting up Chinese Youth Research Cohort (Westlake Adolescent Cohort, WAC), 7775 samples were collected include height, weight and bone mineral density. Blood tests include dozens of indicators (including vitamin D) that may affect bone mineral deposition. It focuses on adolescent growth, adolescent peak bone mineral density.

Research cohort for osteoporosis and fracture

By setting up a network of cooperative hospitals to include specimens of osteoporosis and fracture patients, the results can be used in the prevention and treatment of osteoporosis and fragile fracture in elderly and postmenopausal women.



Research cohort for osteoporosis and fracture:Large research cohort for babies and children

Build a child genome database by collecting store blood, saliva, urine, feces, placental stem cells and other biological samples to form a birth defect disease and stem cell research sharing and service platform. It can play an important role in social fields such as criminal investigation, ancestral and kinship.



“Nation People Difference” project

WAC has included 23 different ethnic groups, such as Han, Zhuang, Yi, Miao and Mongolian. The purpose of the study was to analyze the genetic background differences and disease susceptibility of each ethnic group.



“Surname People Difference” project

The establishment of surname genome database can study the disease susceptibility with similar genetic background. It also can serve in the social fields such as ancestral and kinship, finding relatives, criminal investigation
